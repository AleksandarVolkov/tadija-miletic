Biography

After completing piano, solo singing and music theory at the "Stanković" music school, he graduated in dramaturgy in the class of Siniša Kovačević at the Academy of Arts in Belgrade, and then gain a master's degree in directing in the class of Nikita Milivojević at the Academy of Arts in Novi Sad. He is currently finishing his doctoral studies at the University of Arts in Belgrade.

In December 2011, together with twenty successful young artists, he founded the organization "Cultural element" with which he has had almost a hundred cultural and humanitarian activities so far. Since 2016, he has been the director of the International opera competition "Lazar Jovanović", the largest opera competition in Serbia. Since 2019, he has been an assistant professor at the Faculty of Contemporary Arts in Belgrade, where he also worked as the head of the drama department, and since 2023, he has been an assistant professor at the Belgrade Dance Institute. He is the author of the television series "Prva Tarifa", which premiered on Prva Television.

During the first fifteen years of his career, he worked at the Madlenianum Opera and Theater, where, in addition to directing, assistant directing and dramatization, he performed a number of solo and choral roles in operas and musicals. He was the president of the jury at several literary events and theater festivals. He was a member of the presidency of Serbian Mensa, where he was also the editor-in-chief of the magazine "MozaIQ" for eight years. In addition, he worked for years in the same capacity as editor-in-chief at the Festival of monodrama and mime, as well as at the New fortress theater festival, and for five years he worked as a troupe leader for the BITEF festival. He is the founder of the art education center Cultural Institute.

He actively holds lectures and seminars throughout the country. He created over thirty operas, plays and projects. He gained professional expertise in Monaco at the Monte Carlo Opera and in Nagoya, Japan.
